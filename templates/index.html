About This Application
Driving Profile Classification

The classification models predict whether your driving style is economical or not based on various vehicle parameters like RPM, speed, throttle position, acceleration, and engine load.

Fuel Consumption Prediction

The regression models predict fuel consumption in liters per hour based on the same set of parameters. This helps you understand how your driving behavior affects fuel economy.

Models Used

Classification Models: Logistic Regression, XGBoost, Random Forest, SVM, K-Nearest Neighbors, and Decision Tree
Regression Models: Ridge Regression, XGBoost Regressor, SVR, Random Forest Regressor, and MLPRegressor

Metrics Explained
  • Accuracy: Percentage of correct predictions
  • Precision: Ratio of true positives to all positive predictions
  • Recall: Ratio of true positives to all actual positives
  • F1 Score: Harmonic mean of precision and recall
  • MSE: Mean Squared Error - average of squared differences between predicted and actual values
  • MAE: Mean Absolute Error - average of absolute differences between predicted and actual values
  • RÂ²: Coefficient of determination - proportion of variance in the dependent variable predictable from the independent variables
